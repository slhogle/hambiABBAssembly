1 Introduction

We will use the metacal package for estimating bias and performing calibration in the special case where the bias of all the taxa of interest can be directly measured from the control sample. Since samples extracted by Qiagen and by boil are exactly the same we can estimate scaling factors to produce corrected relative abundance for other samples.

Publication: Consistent and correctable bias in metagenomic sequencing experiments
